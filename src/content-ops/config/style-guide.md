# PRICEIT Style Guide

## Formatting
- **Headlines:** Sentence case, not Title Case. ("Price every job" not "Price Every Job")
- **Numbers:** Always use numerals for measurements ("2 minutes", "3 trades", "60%")
- **Percentages:** Always use % symbol, not "percent"
- **Dollar amounts:** $45K not "$45,000" for round numbers in headlines/hooks
- **Oxford comma:** Always use it

## Product Name
- Always: **PRICEIT** (all caps)
- Never: Priceit, PriceIt, Price It, priceit

## Content Types & Lengths

### LinkedIn post
- Hook: 1–2 lines max, no emoji in line 1
- Body: 3–5 short paragraphs, line breaks between each
- CTA: 1 line, direct
- Total: 150–300 words

### Blog post
- Headline: Under 60 characters
- Intro: Lead with pain or stat, max 2 sentences
- Subheads: Every 200–300 words
- Total: 600–1,200 words (no longer unless SEO-driven)
- End with CTA to beta waitlist

### Email (outreach)
- Subject: Under 50 characters, no clickbait
- Opening line: No "I hope this finds you well"
- Body: 3–5 sentences max per email
- CTA: One ask only per email
- Signature: Name, title, PRICEIT, one link

### Social (Instagram/X)
- Hook in first line
- Max 3 hashtags on Instagram, 0–1 on X
- No generic hashtags (#business #entrepreneur)
- Use construction-specific: #contractor #constructiontech #estimating

## SEO Content Rules
- Target keyword in H1 and first 100 words
- Meta description: 150–160 characters, includes keyword + CTA
- Internal links: At least 1 per post
- Always end with beta signup CTA
